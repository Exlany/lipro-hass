# Code of Conduct

We expect all contributors and users to interact respectfully, constructively, and in good faith.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions.
- Focus on technical facts, reproducible evidence, and actionable feedback.
- Assume good intent, but do not tolerate harassment, abuse, discrimination, or doxxing.
- Keep logs, screenshots, and diagnostics free of sensitive personal data whenever possible.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Publishing private or security-sensitive information without permission
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of review, support, or issue triage workflows

## Reporting

For community conduct issues, contact the maintainer through the support paths documented in `SUPPORT.md`.
For security issues, follow `SECURITY.md` instead of opening a public report.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or block access when behavior violates this policy.
