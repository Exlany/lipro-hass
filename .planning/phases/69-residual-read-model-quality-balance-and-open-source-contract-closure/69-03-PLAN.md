<objective>

Purpose: 落实 `ARC-16 / HOT-26 / HOT-27 / QLT-27`，解决“service 像 protocol compiler、wrapper 像第二入口”的残留。
Output: thinner schedule/service path, explicit wrapper ownership, synchronized baseline/review truth, stronger focused verification.
</objective>
